Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: December 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

RAMA ADVISORY (Australian Credit Licence No. 389328) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and store your personal information in connection with our credit services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details (name, address, phone, email)
  • Financial information (income, expenses, assets, liabilities)
  • Employment details and history
  • Identification documents (driver's licence, passport)
  • Credit-related information and credit history
  • Property and loan preferences
  • Information about your requirements and objectives

2. How We Collect Information

Directly From You

  • Credit application forms
  • Phone conversations and meetings
  • Email correspondence
  • Website enquiry forms
  • Supporting documentation

From Third Parties

  • Credit reporting bodies
  • Lenders and financial institutions
  • Employers (with your consent)
  • Government agencies
  • Professional referrers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Assess your creditworthiness and loan application
  • Provide credit assistance and mortgage broking services
  • Communicate with lenders and other parties on your behalf
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Provide ongoing customer service and support
  • Send you relevant information about our services (with consent)
  • Improve our services and business operations
  • Manage complaints and disputes

4. Information Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information to the following parties:

Credit-Related Parties

  • Banks, credit unions and other lenders
  • Credit reporting bodies
  • Mortgage insurers and guarantors
  • Valuers and settlement agents
  • Credit licensees and representatives

Service Providers

  • Legal and accounting professionals
  • IT and document storage providers
  • Professional advisers and consultants
  • Government agencies (when required by law)
  • External dispute resolution schemes

5. Credit Reporting

We may collect credit information from and disclose credit information to credit reporting bodies in accordance with the Privacy Act. This includes:

  • Credit applications and enquiries
  • Credit history and repayment history information
  • Default information and serious credit infringements
  • Court judgments and bankruptcy information
  • Credit eligibility information for assessment purposes

You have rights regarding your credit information, including the right to access your credit report and request corrections.

6. Data Security and Storage

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure storage systems with restricted access controls
  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Regular security assessments and system updates
  • Staff training on privacy and security obligations
  • Secure disposal of documents when no longer required
  • Physical security measures for paper records

We retain your personal information for as long as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the following rights:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Make a complaint about our privacy practices
  • Request we stop sending direct marketing communications
  • Understand how your information is being used and disclosed
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided in this policy.

8. Website and Digital Services

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Analyse website traffic and usage patterns
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide relevant content and information

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Our website may also contain links to third-party websites with their own privacy policies.
